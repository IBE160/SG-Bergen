How to Play

expand_more
  1. Get your character: At the start of the game, you and your opponent will each be assigned a secret character from the board.
  2. Ask yes/no questions: Take turns asking questions that can only be answered with "yes" or "no" to narrow down the possibilities. (e.g., "Does your character have glasses?").
  3. Guess to win: When you think you know who their character is, make your guess on your turn. If you're right, you win! But be careful, if you're wrong, you lose the game.
